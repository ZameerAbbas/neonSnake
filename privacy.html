Last updated: July 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to NeonSnake ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you visit and play our free browser Snake game at https://easypdf.fit (the "Site").

By using the Site, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the Site.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only minimal data necessary to operate the Site:

  • High Score (localStorage): Your in-game high score is stored locally in your own browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not sent to any server.
  • Usage Data (via Google AdSense / Analytics): Third-party advertising partners (Google) may collect anonymised information about your visit, such as pages viewed, browser type, device type, and IP address.
  • Cookies: We do not set first-party cookies. However, our advertising partner (Google AdSense) may set cookies on your device. See Section 4 for details.

3. How We Use Information

  • To operate and improve the game experience.
  • To display advertisements via Google AdSense to support free access to the Site.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Google AdSense & Cookies

This Site uses Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC. Google AdSense uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website and other sites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings .

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our Site, please visit: How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps .

You can also opt out of third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices or www.youronlinechoices.eu (EU users).

5. Third-Party Services

Our Site uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdSense — Advertising. Google Privacy Policy

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to access — Request a copy of data held about you.
  • Right to deletion — Request deletion of your data.
  • Right to opt out — Opt out of personalised ads (see Section 4).
  • Clear localStorage — You can clear your high score at any time by opening your browser's Developer Tools → Application → Local Storage and deleting the neonsnake_highscore_v1 key.

California residents (CCPA): We do not sell personal information. Google AdSense data sharing is subject to Google's own CCPA compliance.

7. Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect your information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. The game stores only your high score in your local browser storage and does not transmit personal data to our servers.
